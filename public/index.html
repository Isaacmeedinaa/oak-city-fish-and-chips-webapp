OUR HISTORY

LEARN ABOUT US

Isaac B. Horton IV is a 7th generation Raleighite. His drive to cook fresh and delicious seafood is a family tradition. Fresh seafood, fried to perfection, and seasoned with two of Isaac’s very own proprietary spice blends is what Oak City Fish And Chips endeavors to establish for the city, a staple for amazing seafood. To do so in conjunction with the Morgan Street Food Hall & Market is a powerful illustration of true southern flavor joining forces with phenomenal creativity in Downtown Raleigh.
